How the Parking Lot Works

The parking lot is a living list of candidate practices — not a fixed roadmap. Items enter from four sources, get fleshed out collaboratively, and are prioritized based on data and organizational need. Nothing in the lot is committed until Citizens and Flint agree to move it forward.
