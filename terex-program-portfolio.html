The Flint framework

Where training ends,
behavior begins.

Behavior change in the flow of work happens through long-running Programs — each composed of 90-day Initiatives, each delivered by short, situational Activities that reach every level between the individual contributor and the senior leader, in the flow of their actual work. Here is how Terex is using that framework across five programs running in parallel.

01

Programs are durable

A Program — Safety, Manufacturing SOPs, Accountability — runs indefinitely. It's the strategic frame. The behavior outcomes compound year over year as new initiatives ladder onto previous ones.

02

Initiatives cycle every 90 days

Within each Program, a focused 3-month Initiative tackles one concrete behavioral lever — PPE compliance, peer feedback, prompt fundamentals. New Initiatives queue up perpetually behind the active one.

03

Activities live in the flow of work

The Initiative gets delivered through small Flint Activities — daily huddles, end-of-shift checks, real-time field coaching prompts — that integrate into the existing rhythm rather than pulling people offline.
