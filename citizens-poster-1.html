Pushing one level deeper
How the coach moves from surface observation to root cause
Surface answer
The learner names what they can see
“It is a busy branch and carpets get dirty with traffic.”
Learner’s first response
Coach move
Acknowledge, then press
“That’s a reasonable starting point, but push it one level deeper — is the issue that cleaning frequency isn’t keeping up with the traffic?”
Coach challenges the frame
Root cause
The real, actionable diagnosis
“Cleaning frequency needs to match the traffic level at this branch.”
Learner now owns the hypothesis
The shift
“Without a why, there’s no clear path to a fix.”
