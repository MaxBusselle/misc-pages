What the before-and-after says

Learner and manager views, read together
Learners

Confidence rose most on Reaching customers who don't pick up (+17%) — the proactive-outreach behaviour the initiative set out to change. Securing payment or a promise still finished lowest of the three (7.1), so it stayed the hardest part of the job.

Managers

Managers rated the same collectors a little lower throughout, and also placed Securing at the bottom (6.5). Their send-backs clustered on one thing: promises made without a specific payment date.

Learners and managers land in the same place: securing a keepable, dated promise is the lagging skill — the very gap the manager send-backs point to — and the clear focus for continued coaching.

What we changed — and will change — because of what the feedback showed. Every item names the exact thing being changed, at one of five levels: an activity, a workplace practice, an initiative, the program, or the coaching context for a specific group.

ActivityShipped · week 6
Work a Cold File End-to-End

Before the first dial, the activity now requires two checks in ASSET: the Parties tab — references, co-signer, and originating merchant — and the payment history with prior call dispositions.

Triggered by: 58 misfires — collectors dialed cold without reviewing the file.

Workplace practiceShipped · week 4
Reaching customers who don't pick up

Coaching now walks through disclosure limits before any reference call: locate purpose only, and never mention the debt.

Triggered by: 41 misfires — reference calls drifted toward disclosure.

Workplace practiceShipped · week 8
Reaching customers who don't pick up

Every legitimacy opening now ends by confirming the customer's best phone number and email, so each conversation refreshes the file.

Triggered by: a vetted Possible Byproduct — 31 reports of customers updating their own contact details during openings.

Coaching contextShipped · week 7
Vertical: Retail and Health accounts

For accounts with no merchant-purchase anchor, coaching supplies alternate ways to open with legitimacy.

Triggered by: barrier reports concentrated in accounts without a memorable purchase to reference.

Coaching contextPlanned · Initiative 2
Location: London

Coaching for the London floor adds a protected daily outbound block and prompts for manager check-ins.

Triggered by: an engagement lag — 82% active and 5.6 activities per learner, against 91% and 7.1 overall.

InitiativeShipped · week 9
Proactive Cash Collection

Reaching guidance now includes texting from your direct line before a third call attempt.

Triggered by: a vetted Candidate Best Practice. 31 collectors have adopted it so far.

ProgramPlanned · Initiative 2
LendCare Collections

Every future initiative's coaching will open customer contact with the legitimacy anchor: your name, LendCare, and the merchant purchase.

Triggered by: 89 barrier reports of customers screening calls from a company they don't recognize.
