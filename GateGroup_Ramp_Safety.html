Activity Snapshot · Supervisor Practice
Targeted Ramp Observation: Priority Driver
Before heading airside, identify one driver from your SQOR log with a repeat at-risk behavior. Plan your observation around their next flight.
1
Review SQOR log; flag a driver with 2+ recorded observations for the same behavior.
2
Position to observe during their next ground operation; note whether the behavior recurs.
3
If it does: deliver a verbal warning referencing the prior flags. Log the interaction.
