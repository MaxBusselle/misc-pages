developing its sales team.

Why Behaviors Weren't Sticking
Yet the habits weren't
translating in the field.
Reactive selling as the default Under pressure, reps respond to what customers ask for rather than leading the conversation
Manager variability How much a rep develops depends heavily on the relative strength of their individual manager
The pace of the job Traveling, busy — advanced skill-building loses out to getting through the week
