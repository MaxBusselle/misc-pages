Documented Performance Required

QMSR doesn't just require procedures—it requires evidence that personnel are competent and that training was effective. This is a fundamental shift from the old regulation.

155 Function-Specific Practices Evidence of Effectiveness
⚠️

The Knowing-Doing Gap

Traditional training proves attendance, not competence. A training certificate shows someone showed up—it doesn't prove the training changed how they actually work.

ISO 13485 Clause 6.2 "Evaluate effectiveness of actions taken"

The Core Question

Traditional training answers: "Did you tell them what to do?"
QMSR asks: "Did you change how they actually work?" — and demands proof.
