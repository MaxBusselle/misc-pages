Directing Account Focus

How the coaching progression works

Practice 1
3

Coaching moves.

Not thirteen activities.

Each milestone teaches a qualitatively different coaching conversation. The activities within each level are scaffolding — easier and harder versions of the same move.

Three different coaching conversations

Basic

Reviewing Account Selections

Ask which Assigned Accounts your PASA is calling this week and push for data-based reasoning.

"Walk me through why those accounts."
Intermediate

Coaching Pre-Call Preparation

Before a call, verify the PASA reviewed purchase history and has specific, data-informed topics ready.

"Show me what you looked at to prepare."
Advanced

Redirecting Misallocated Effort

Spot a PASA investing time in a flat account, show them the data, and redirect them to one with a clear sales opportunity.

"Are you seeing a good return on this account?"

Summary

Progression the DS can feel.

→Three qualitatively different coaching conversations — not one move repeated thirteen times
→Both the DS coaching move and the PASA capability visibly escalate at every step
→Scaffolding within each level — every DS has an on-ramp and a stretch target
→Routing based on demonstrated behavior — no one stuck, no one skips what they need
→Pro DS employees who finish Practice 1 early can move to Practice 2 or take a timed break
→Practices 2 and 3 ready to build as fast-follow once the data shows readiness
