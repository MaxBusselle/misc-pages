Customer Success Meeting Modules

Quick Reference · Flint Learning Solutions · Internal Use

🚀
Launch
Initiative Kickoff & Onboarding Readiness
One-Time Operational Early

Confirm the initiative is launching cleanly, surface early blockers, and send the sponsor away with a clear action list and committed follow-up steps.

Agenda
  • Access & Enrollment Status — on track for 80–90% by week one?
  • Technical Issues — problems and resolution plan
  • Onboarding Materials — distribution confirmed?
💚
Pulse
Very Early Insights
One-Time Relational Early Executive

Relieve early anxiety. Replace "will they adopt it?" with specific, visible evidence that the initiative is alive and working.

Agenda
  • Early Activity Completion — who's in, what are they saying?
  • Participant Sentiment — early experience with the platform
  • Coach Engagement — what early interactions look like
⚓
Baseline
Baseline Survey Insights
One-Time Insight Early Executive

Show where participants are starting from. Begin the narrative arc that pays off in DELTA. Establish which focus areas matter most to the sponsor.

Agenda
  • Competency Snapshot — participant & manager ratings
  • Where the Gaps Are — lowest-scoring areas
  • Manager vs. Participant View — where they diverge
🤖
Coach
The Role of the AI Coach
Repeats Insight Mid

Demonstrate that the platform is actively learning and responding — not just delivering activities. Give sponsors insight into their workforce they couldn't get elsewhere.

Agenda
  • Coach Focus Areas — what is it consistently addressing?
  • Participant-Coach Dynamics — how are people responding?
  • What the Coach Is Teaching Us — knowledge gaps, resistance, value
🎯
Themes
Emerging Themes from Activity Data
Repeats Insight Mid Executive

Move the conversation from activity counts to real organizational intelligence. Surface patterns the sponsor recognizes from their own experience leading this workforce.

Agenda
  • Top Themes — most frequent topics across feedback
  • Obstacles to Excellence — where participants are stuck
  • Bright Spots — strong outcomes and unexpected wins
🧭
North Star
Tying Data to Strategic Goals
Repeats Strategic Mid Executive

Elevate from metrics to impact. Give sponsors language and data they can use to report up internally — and confidence that the investment is delivering.

Agenda
  • Goals Recap — stated objectives at initiative start
  • Evidence of Progress — data tied directly to those goals
  • Gaps & Risks — where we may fall short of the vision
🔍
Excavate
Root Cause & Opportunity Analysis
One-Time Insight Strategic Late Executive

Deliver insight the sponsor couldn't generate on their own. Show that the platform reveals why behavior was stuck, not just whether it changed.

Agenda
  • Primary Blockers — systemic obstacles to adoption
  • Underlying Root Causes — environment, culture, capability gaps
  • Highest-Leverage Opportunities — where intervention matters most
🌅
Horizon
Program Expansion & Next Initiatives
One-Time Strategic Late Expansion Executive

Shift the sponsor from "running a pilot" to "building a program." Use this initiative's data as the springboard for a longer-term partnership conversation.

Agenda
  • Version 2.0 — what should we do differently next run?
  • Adjacent Initiatives — natural next steps in this program area
  • Broader Program Vision — what does the full program look like?
📊
Delta
Behavior Change Results
One-Time Insight Closing Executive

The definitive proof of impact. Give sponsors data they can stand behind — and the confidence to present it upward and make the case for continued investment.

Agenda
  • Participant Self-Report Delta — baseline to follow-up shift
  • Manager Assessment Delta — how managers rate the change
  • Headline Results — the one-sentence story this data tells
