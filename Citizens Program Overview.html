See It, Own It, Fix It

An ongoing program to build a lasting culture of quality ownership across Citizens REO. Focused quarterly initiatives target specific roles, specific problems, and measurable outcomes — evolving as the data and priorities evolve.

🔄

Ongoing Program

Not a one-time training event. A sustained commitment to quality culture that runs continuously, with no fixed end date.

🎯

Quarterly Initiatives

Each quarter, focused initiatives target specific QC problems, role groups, or operational challenges. Multiple can run in parallel.

📈

Data-Driven Selection

Initiative priorities are chosen by QC inspection scores, prior results, and leadership direction — not guesswork.

How It Works
How Initiative Priorities Are Chosen
We don't predetermine what matters a year from now. A consistent process selects the highest-impact initiatives each quarter based on real data and real conditions.
1
Review QC Data

Pull updated inspection scores. Identify items that remain low, improved items, and newly emerging problem areas.

›
2
Assess Prior Results

Evaluate the most recent initiative. Did targeted scores improve? Which behaviors stuck? Where do gaps remain?

›
3
Gather Leadership Input

Consult senior leaders on emerging priorities, customer feedback, regional challenges, and strategic direction.

›
4
Design & Launch

Select target audience, scope, practice areas, and success metrics. Build the initiative and launch for the quarter.

Inputs Shaping Each Quarter's Focus
QC Inspection Scores

Updated data showing which items remain lowest-scoring and where new gaps have emerged.

Prior Initiative Outcomes

Which scores moved, which didn't, and what behavioral changes were observed from the last cycle.

Leadership Direction

Strategic priorities from senior leadership — executive concerns, customer feedback, organizational focus.

Field Feedback

What FMs and participants report from the ground — recurring frustrations, vendor issues, seasonal patterns.

Audience Readiness

Which role groups have completed prior initiatives and which haven't been engaged yet.

Operational Calendar

Seasonal timing, budget cycles, and competing organizational demands that affect what's possible.

Audiences
Role Groups Across the Program
Different initiatives engage different role groups. All audiences are role-based, not department-based.
🛠 Facility Managers & FM Leads

Frontline leaders responsible for building condition and quality. Manage maintenance technicians, janitors, painters, and field staff.

~50 people — Facility Managers, Senior FMs, Landscape FMs
🏢 Pod Members

Cross-functional project teams. Six specialized roles collaborating on branch construction, renovation, and real estate projects.

6 roles per pod — Senior PM, Junior PM, Property Analyst, Retail Designer, Environmental Designer, Leasing Partner
💼 Senior Leaders

Direct reports to the Head of Real Estate Operations. Set standards, hold teams accountable, and drive culture from the top.

Direct reports to Chris Caso
📋 Administrative & Strategy Roles

Staff who influence quality through processes, data, documentation, and cross-functional coordination.

Varies — Property PMs, Property Analysts, Strategy roles
