Program IntroductionWhat this is and why it matters

Citizens Skills Coach is built around a single goal: helping the REO team catch quality issues before customers do. FMs already know the quality standards — the gap isn't knowledge, it's building the deliberate habit of fresh-eye observation so small issues get caught early.

The program delivers that through bite-size activities designed to fit directly into your regular work over the course of several weeks. Think of it less as training and more as a personal job coach: a structured, ongoing nudge that keeps you intentional and sharp in the work you're already doing.

2.2📋 Completing the Initial Questionnaire

The first thing you'll see is a short questionnaire. It personalises your experience — it is not a test or a performance review.

⭐ Remember: a rating of 5 is good!

You are not being scored or compared to anyone else. Be as honest as you can — the more accurate your answers, the better your activities will match where you actually are right now.

  • There are no right or wrong answers.
  • The whole questionnaire takes around 5–10 minutes.
2.3👔 Information for Managers

If you manage participants in this program, you're an active part of making it work — not just an observer. Here's what's on your plate.

📋 Your Questionnaire — Two Rounds

You'll complete this questionnaire twice: once now as a baseline, and once at the end to measure progress.

  • Round 1 — Now: Your baseline read on each participant's current habits and skills.
  • Round 2 — End of Initiative: A follow-up after the program — what progress did you see?
  • Complete one questionnaire per team member enrolled — both rounds.
  • Takes about 5–10 minutes per person. Go with your honest first instinct.

📊 Weekly Status Updates

Each week you'll get an email showing which of your team members have overdue activities. A quick check-in nudge is often all it takes.

📌 Important: You'll also receive a follow-up questionnaire

When participants finish the program, both they and their managers get a follow-up questionnaire. This is critical for measuring impact — please complete it when it arrives.

💬 Make the most of the AI Chat

Don't just read and move on — use the chat to ask questions, explore ideas, and work through how things apply to your specific situation. There are no wrong questions, and the more you engage, the more useful it gets.

🌟 Your feedback shapes this program for everyone

When you close an activity, describe what you actually did and give honest feedback — don't skip this step. Your responses go directly to the Citizens and Flint team and shape future activities for FMs across Citizens. Your voice matters.
