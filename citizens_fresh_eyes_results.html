That the System Is Working.

A look at engagement, field outcomes, and what the coaching data shows about behavior change across our branches.

01 — The Challenge

"Repeat Blindness"

Our people are in branches every day. Familiarity is the enemy — issues that have always been there stop being seen by the people best positioned to fix them.

The problem isn't that our people don't care. It's that the eyes adjust. We need a way to reset that — so our people are seeing what's there with fresh eyes.

— Chris Caso, SVP Head of Real Estate Operations
02 — The Initiative

Fresh Eyes: Seeing What Needs Attention

Short, on-the-job activities — done inside visits FMs are already making. Each one is coached individually and builds on the last. The progression moves from basic to more advanced skills.

I
Observe & Document
Walk a branch zone like a first-time customer. Log what you find in 360.
II
Stay Alert on Work Order Visits
Don't just close the ticket you came for. Document what you find beyond it.
III
Prepare Before You Arrive
Review 360 first. Arrive knowing which issues are aging and what to look for.
IV
Diagnose the Recurring Issue
Find what keeps coming back. Identify why. Assign an owner and resolution date before you leave.
V
Complete the Customer-Path Audit
Walk the full location exterior-to-exit. Rank findings by customer impact. P1s actioned before you leave.
